Why this fits REI specifically

Four reasons we think the conversation is worth having

01

Your buyers are already in the network.

Shop Pay's 250M+ recognized buyers skew heavily toward outdoor and active categories — the same audience filling REI carts on a Saturday morning. They're recognized the moment they hit checkout. No password, no form, no friction.

02

Mobile is where the math really moves.

Shop Pay's biggest lift shows up on mobile, where typing addresses on a phone is the single largest source of cart abandonment. For an REI customer browsing during a hike or on a lunch break, one-tap checkout is the difference between a sale and a "later."

03

Installments fits a high-ticket assortment.

A $2,400 e-bike or a $700 tent is exactly where Shop Pay Installments earns its keep — lifting AOV without REI taking on credit risk. Merchants regularly see 25–50% AOV gains on eligible orders.

04

It's the only carbon-neutral way to pay online.

Every Shop Pay order has its emissions offset through Shopify's Sustainability Fund. For a co-op whose members already opt outside on Black Friday, the values fit isn't a stretch — it's the most natural payment story REI could tell.

What it actually takes

This isn't a replatform conversation.

Shop Pay can be added as a payment option alongside REI's existing checkout stack via Shop Pay Component or Shop Pay Checkout — both designed for non-Shopify merchants. Typical integrations land in 4–8 weeks with a small dedicated team. Shopify's integration team partners directly through go-live.
